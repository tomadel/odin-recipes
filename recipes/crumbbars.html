Blueberry Crumb Bars

Home

Description

It is cheap and easy to make crumb bars. Everybody love to eat them. You can use any berries to make crumb bars!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup shortening
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ teaspoon salt (Optional)
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon (Optional)
  • 4 cups fresh blueberries
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 3 teaspoons cornstarch

Steps

  1. Step

    Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch pan.

  2. Step

    In a medium bowl, stir together 1 cup sugar, 3 cups flour, and baking powder. Mix in salt and cinnamon, if desired.

    Use a fork or pastry cutter to blend in the shortening and egg. Dough will be crumbly. Pat half of dough into the prepared pan.

  3. Step

    In another bowl, stir together the sugar and cornstarch. Gently mix in the blueberries.

    Sprinkle the blueberry mixture evenly over the crust. Crumble remaining dough over the berry layer.

  4. Step

    Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes, or until top is slightly brown. Cool completely before cutting into squares.

    5. Enjoy Your Blueberry Crumb Bars!
